David Muchilee

Actor || Digital Marketer || Model || Traveller.

David Adjei Owusu known by his brand name David Muchilee is a Digital Marketer, Actor, Model and a Travel Consultant. He holds a Computer Science Degree from the prestigious Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology(KNUST), Kumasi, Ashanti, Ghana.

David Muchilee is the CEO of Muchilee Travel Services, a Travel Consultancy Firm that offers expert advices on visa applications to the UK, Schengen Area, South Africa, Dubai, etc.
He presently works as a Social Media Manager for Uniemploy International, Investor HuB and Customer Connect.

With hands on experience, David Muchilee has actively represented brands like ADB Bank, Bank of Africa, Dalex Finance, Bel-Cola and Royal Drinks to mention but a few to an acceptable standard of serviceability.
As an Actor with active media presence he has featured in a couple of TV Series such as PUZZLED, Sakumono, etc.

As a Digital Strategiest and marketer, i follow a user-centric brand approach with core tactics on digital and social media strategies for brands which plays a critical role in leading brands and ambitious organizations to new heights of customer engagement and business success. More specifically, my work is the intersection of insight-driven customer engagement, informed channel selection, content marketing strategy, optimal experience and brand management.
